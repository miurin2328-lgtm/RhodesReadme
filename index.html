Rhodes, on the lands of the Wangal people of the Eora Nation, sits along the Parramatta River in the City of Canada Bay. Today, its riverside pathways, parks, and dog-friendly spaces draw locals and visitors for walking, cycling, picnics, and sunsets, with easy access from nearby shops and transport. As part of the Parramatta River Heritage Walk, Rhodes offers both a vibrant community hub for recreation and a gateway to the river’s deeper cultural and historical stories.
